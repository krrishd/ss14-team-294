About This Game

The game you see here is a prototype of a broader concept called Elasticity

The concept behind Elasticity is to use video games to encourage people to donate real money to charity. This prototype shows what such a game might look like, but does not involve donating real money.

How to Play

You start the game off with a certain number of credits, which you can use to purchase items from the Marketplace. Items from the Marketplace are designed to accelerate the growth of your village. The items you buy from the Marketplace work in your village and give you more credits in return.

Some of the items in the Marketplace give a better return on investment than others. Part of the fun is finding the fastest way to make your city grow.

For a Better Cause

The items you buy correspond to actual loan categories on Kiva. When you "buy" virtual items, the game looks up loans on Kiva that you might be interested in.
